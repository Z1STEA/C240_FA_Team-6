About

CampusAI is an AI-powered Republic Polytechnic FAQ Assistant built using Botpress and a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) Knowledge Base. It helps students quickly find information on admissions, courses, fees, campus facilities, student life and more through natural language conversations.

Features

🎓 Admissions

Application guides and admission information.

📚 Courses

Explore RP diplomas and schools.

🏫 Campus

Find classrooms and facilities easily.
